Frequently Asked Questions
What is an NFT?
A unique digital asset on a blockchain, representing ownership of items like art or collectibles.
What can I use NFTs for?
Digital art, gaming, music, virtual real estate, and royalties.
What is the difference between an NFT and cryptocurrency?
NFTs are unique (non-fungible); cryptocurrencies are interchangeable (fungible).
How much is an NFT worth?
Depends on rarity, creator, and demand—can range from a few dollars to millions.
How do I purchase an NFT on your platform ?
Set up a wallet, fund it with crypto, connect to the platform, browse, and buy with a confirmed transaction. Check your platform’s guide for specifics.
